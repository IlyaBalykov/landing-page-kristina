Kristina Balykova

I am a Ph.D. student in the Linguistics Department of the University of Texas at Austin. Since 2017, I have been doing linguistic fieldwork with two endangered languages spoken in Brazil, Wa'ikhana (East Tukano) and Guató (isolate). My Ph.D. project aims at describing Guató morphosyntax.

I was born in Yoshkar-Ola, Russia. In 2011, I moved to Brazil.
